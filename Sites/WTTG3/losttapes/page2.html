LostTapes.0021

'The Mind Cannot Unsee the Seen.'

The Cow Factory - 2010, 1 hour 22 minutes

Fictional movie about an imagined world where humans and domestic cattle switch places and humans are subject to the same treatment as cows are in factory farms and dairy farms. Banned due to obscenity laws and opposition from the beef and dairy lobby

Puppet Town Life - 1991, 27 minutes

Never-aired children's TV show pilot about a group of puppet characters that go on adventures in real-world locations to learn about tough jobs. Discarded when a dead body was accidentally caught on film while the puppets interviewed a sewer maintenance technician

Arcana: The Last Element - 2018, 2 hours 2 minutes

Never-before-seen multi-million dollar modern fantasy film that never made it out of the gates after a portion of its former A-List cast, director, and executives were charged with unrelated crimes and fell into controversy, all references to this movie's production have been scrubbed. Attained through private connections in Hollywood

Fluffy Objects - 2016, 52 minutes

Camcorder footage of a student group daring each other to progressively more difficult taxidermy challenges. Excessive gore and improper use of lethal injections. Ends with detailed and informative taxidermy on the losers. Submitted anonymously

Heehaw - 1988, 1 hour 33 minutes

R-rated Film about a talking donkey who falls in love with a human woman. Banned in all markets for obvious reasons

SubSolar - 1999, 1 hour 47 minutes

Science fiction film that never made it to editing due to a catastrophic freak accident on set in which almost every member of the cast and crew died in an electrical fire. Footage of the fire, and the deaths, can be seen at the end before the recording cuts off

Nursemaid - 1989, 1 hour 27 minutes

Movie based on a real person employed to take care of patients at a New England asylum but secretly abused them. Movie was banned when it was discovered that the subject of the film was still alive and sued the director for slander

Apterygial - 2007, 1 hour 49 minutes

Horror film about a psychopath who tortures victims by surgically removing their vocal cords, arms, and legs and keeping them in a massive terrarium like pet snakes. Banned due to graphic depictions of nonconsensual surgery and gratuitous violencer
